## 4. Booking Appointments

### 4.1 How Bookings Work

When you book an appointment via HospiJunction:
- You pay a **non-refundable Platform Fee of ₹20** (inclusive of GST) to HospiJunction at the time of booking
- The **consultation fee** is paid directly to the hospital in cash or another method accepted by the hospital, at the time of your visit
- HospiJunction does NOT collect, hold, or process consultation fees on behalf of any hospital

### 4.2 Confirmation

A confirmed appointment is contingent on:
- Successful payment of the Platform Fee
- Availability of the chosen doctor and slot
- The doctor remaining available on the appointment date (e.g., not on emergency leave)

### 4.3 Rescheduling

You may **reschedule an appointment without paying an additional Platform Fee** as long as you do so before your appointment time, subject to slot availability.

### 4.4 Cancellation

If you cancel your appointment:
- The Platform Fee of ₹20 is **non-refundable**
- No additional charges apply

If the hospital or doctor cancels your appointment (e.g., due to doctor leave, emergency, or hospital closure):
- You are entitled to a **full refund of the Platform Fee** (₹20)
- The refund is processed automatically within 5–7 business days to your original payment method

See our [Refund Policy](#refund-policy) for complete details.
