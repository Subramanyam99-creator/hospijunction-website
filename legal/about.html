About HospiJunction

HospiJunction is an Indian healthcare technology platform built to make doctor appointments easier for everyone — patients, hospitals, and doctors.

Our Mission

Healthcare in India is often a struggle of long queues, broken phones, and uncertain waits. HospiJunction exists to fix that. We believe booking a doctor should be as simple as ordering food online — predictable, transparent, and respectful of your time.

What We Do

For patients, we provide:

  • A directory of verified hospitals near you
  • Real-time slot booking with chosen doctors
  • A live queue tracker so you know exactly when your turn is coming
  • Push notifications so you never miss your appointment
  • Easy rescheduling and family member booking

For hospitals, we provide:

  • A digital queue management system
  • Patient appointment dashboards
  • Doctor schedule and leave management
  • Automated patient communication
  • Reduced phone calls and walk-in chaos

How We Make Money

We charge a small ₹20 platform fee per booking (inclusive of GST). That's it. We don't:

  • Take a commission from doctors or hospitals
  • Inflate consultation fees
  • Sell your data to anyone

Consultation fees are paid directly to the hospital, by you, in person.

Our Promise

  • Verified hospitals only — every hospital is manually reviewed before going live
  • No fake reviews — only patients with confirmed visits can leave reviews
  • No spam — we send notifications only about your bookings
  • Privacy first — your data stays yours, full stop

Built In India, For India

We are a registered Indian company, run by a small team based in Chittoor. We comply with India's IT Act, DPDP Act 2023, and consumer protection laws.
