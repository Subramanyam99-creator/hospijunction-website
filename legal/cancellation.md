# Cancellation Policy

## 1. How to Cancel

You can cancel an appointment any time before the appointment date and time:

1. Open the HospiJunction app
2. Tap **Bookings** in the bottom navigation
3. Select the appointment you wish to cancel
4. Tap **Cancel Appointment**
5. Optionally, provide a reason (helps us improve)
6. Confirm

The cancellation is immediate. The hospital is notified automatically.

## 2. Cancellation Fees

| Who Cancels | Platform Fee (₹20) | Consultation Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Patient (you) | Non-refundable | Not charged |
| Hospital | Refunded in full | Not charged |

The ₹20 Platform Fee is **non-refundable** if you cancel. See our [Refund Policy](#refund-policy) for details.

## 3. Recommendation: Reschedule Instead

If you simply want to change the time, use **Reschedule** in the app — it is **free of charge** and avoids losing the Platform Fee.

To reschedule:
1. Open **Bookings** → select your booking
2. Tap **Reschedule**
3. Choose a new date and time slot
4. Confirm

You can reschedule multiple times at no cost.

## 4. No-Show Policy

If you do not show up for an appointment without cancelling in advance:
- The Platform Fee is forfeited (same as a cancellation)
- The hospital may flag your account
- Repeated no-shows may result in account suspension

We recommend cancelling at least 1 hour before your appointment if you cannot attend.

## 5. Cancellation by Hospital

A hospital may cancel your appointment in cases of:
- Doctor going on leave
- Emergency in the hospital
- Operational issues

In all such cases, your **Platform Fee is refunded in full** (see Section 2). You will be notified immediately via push notification and the in-app bell.
