How it works

Three steps. Honestly, that's it.

01

Find a hospital

Browse verified hospitals near you. See doctors, OPD timings, and consultation fees — all in one place.

02

Pick your slot

Choose a time that suits you. Pay the ₹20 platform fee securely via Razorpay. Get a stable token number instantly.

03

Walk in & consult

Show your token at the hospital, see the doctor, pay the consultation fee directly to them. Done.

Pricing

One flat fee. Nothing hidden.

We charge a ₹20 platform fee per booking — inclusive of GST. That's it. The consultation fee goes directly to the hospital, in person, just like it always has.

  • No subscription. No hidden charges.
  • No commission on consultation fees.
  • Hospital cancels? Full ₹20 refund, automatically.
  • Reschedule anytime before your slot — no extra fee.
